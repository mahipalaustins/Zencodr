Our Hiring Process

We've designed a streamlined process that respects your time and showcases your skills

1

Application

Submit your resume, GitHub profile, or portfolio. We value practical skills and experience over formal credentials.

2

Technical Assessment

Complete a short take-home challenge or live coding session (30-90 minutes) relevant to your role.

3

Culture Interview

Meet with team members to discuss your experience, our values, and how we work together.

4

Final Interview

Connect with leadership and discuss your potential impact on our team and product.
